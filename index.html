Meet Laura Hunter

Laura has spent over 40 years working professionally with animals. She's not just another dog trainer—she's one of the most qualified trainers in the Greater Toronto Area.

She specializes in the exact challenges you're facing: fearful dogs, reactive dogs, and Aussies who struggle to settle no matter what you try.

For the past eight years, Laura has continuously studied fear-based reactivity to stay current with the latest science-based research. She knows what works because she's helped thousands of dogs and their owners go from overwhelmed to confident.

Her approach is different. No harsh corrections. No dominance theory. Just proven, positive reinforcement methods that help dogs (and their humans) thrive.
